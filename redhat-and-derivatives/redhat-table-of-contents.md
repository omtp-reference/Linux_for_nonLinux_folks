## Red Hat and Derivatives

Fedora is the flagship of the Red Hat family for desktop users — cutting-edge,
community-driven, and the proving ground for what eventually lands in Red Hat
Enterprise Linux. If you want a polished Linux desktop that stays current without
going full rolling-release, Fedora is an excellent choice.

### A Note on the Red Hat Family

| Distro                              | Who It's For                                                |
| ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Fedora**                          | Desktop users, developers, people who want current software |
| **RHEL** (Red Hat Enterprise Linux) | Businesses — stable, supported, paid                        |
| **CentOS Stream**                   | Developers tracking what will eventually be in RHEL         |
| **Rocky Linux / AlmaLinux**         | Free RHEL rebuilds — what CentOS used to be                 |

If a friend or family member asks you what to use, Fedora Workstation is the answer.
The others are server and enterprise territory.
